About Bahri

Why Choose Us, We Supply Quality Marine Products.

Al Baraka Group was founded by Mr.Moosa Moham- mad Aqil Abbas (Chairman), who built a massive Commercial organization in the Trading and Ship- chandling field decades ago. Since 2010, Al Baraka Shipchandling services have been a by-product of the Company's growth. We were then acknowledged as one of the Fastest-Growing Companies in the Marine Ship Supply Industry with the best Quality products supplied at most Competitive Rates. We have achieved remarkable growth while meeting the High Standards and Requirements of both Local and International Clients.

What We Do! VISION & MISSION

To be the Go-To Shipchandling Service Company for On / Offshore Industries by providing Dependable and High-Quality supplies while emphasiz- ing Safety, Health, Quality & Environ- mental sustainability.
